Everything in one place

Stop stitching together five different systems. Case notes, timesheets, payroll, housing, and funder reports all live in one platform, so the numbers match and nobody re-keys data.

Case Management

Intake, enrollments, service tracking, assessments, and progress notes, all tied to the client record and the funding source paying for it.

Housing Management

Properties, units, beds, placements, waitlists, inspections, and subsidy tracking, for both shelter-style and apartment-style housing.

HR & Payroll

Employee records, time tracking, scheduling, payroll processing, and credential management. Hours auto-link to the grants funding the positions.

Fund Accounting

Track every dollar by funding source. Manage budgets, expenses, revenue, and generate the financial reports your funders actually need.

Compliance & Documents

Consent forms, e-signatures, Release of Information tracking, HIPAA audit trails, and automatic expiration alerts. Built for regulated environments.

Reporting & Analytics

Custom reports that pull from any data source in the system. Multi-source joins, scheduled reports, and dashboards designed for grant reporting.

Where the dollars actually go

Every service a staff member logs, every hour they work, and every dollar spent traces back to the specific grant or contract that's paying for it.

So when a funder report comes due, the numbers are already there. No last-minute spreadsheets, no reconciling five systems by hand.
